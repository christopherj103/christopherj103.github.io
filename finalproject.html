Final Project

Take a look at what I did for my final project!

For my final project, I coded a game of hangman. The goal is to try to guess the hidden word by clicking on letters that it could contain. For every correctly guessed letter, it will be displayed on the blank spaces. If all letters are correctly guessed before the hangman is finished, then the game is won. However, for every incorrect guess, a new body part on hangman is drawn. Once all of the body parts are drawn (the head, torso, both arms, and both legs), the game is over and the word will be revealed. Once the game is won or lost, you will be given the option to play again with a new word. Good luck!
